Merck · Pembrolizumab + Enzalutamide · mCRPC · Phase III · Discontinued March 2023

"The monitoring architecture was designed for a different kind of drug. Here is the system I would have built instead."

The Architecture of Failure

Cytotoxic chemotherapy assumptions applied to immune checkpoint biology

Crack 2

Futility Boundary Blind to Immunotherapy Temporality

Standard conditional power assumed constant hazard. Immunotherapy does not live in constant time. The DMC stopped at 50% events — before late separation could emerge.

Intervention A

Adaptive Futility Boundary

Asymmetric boundaries. More patient early (40% events). Less forgiving late (70% events). Time-varying conditional power with delayed effect parameter.

ICH E9 · ICH E9(R1)
FDA Adaptive Design Guidance

Crack 4

Safety Monitoring Blind to Safety-as-Efficacy Modifier

Discontinuation is not tolerability. It is efficacy erosion wearing a safety costume. A third of patients exited before the drug could answer for itself.

Intervention B

Integrated Safety-Efficacy Monitoring

Dose-intensity dashboard. IPCW-adjusted survival analysis. Grade-stratified rechallenge algorithm. Safety data reviewed as structural efficacy modifiers.
