Door 01 — Neurology / Regulatory Affairs

Aduhelm

Biogen/Eisai · Anti-Amyloid Monoclonal Antibody · Alzheimer's Disease · Phase III · Accelerated Approval June 2021

"The building passed site inspection while the foundation was already compromised. The inspection measures what it was designed to measure. It does not measure what it was not designed to look for."

115 meetings between FDA and Biogen in 12 months. 66 unrecorded.

The Architecture of Collapse

Independence became optional. Once optional, the system began producing something that looked like science and behaved like marketing.

Floor 01

The Post-Hoc Salvage

The DMC recommended futility. Biogen claimed analytical error. The DMC was never reconvened. No independent verification.

Floor 02

The Firewall Collapse

115 meetings. 66 unrecorded. Joint briefing documents drafted by FDA staff. The distance between sponsor and regulator quietly dissolved.

Floor 03

The Advisory Committee Bypass

10 of 11 voted against approval. FDA approved anyway. The pathway was constructed around the committee, not through it.

Floor 04

The Surrogate Endpoint Substitution

Approved on amyloid reduction — a surrogate. The clinical endpoint the trial was designed to measure had already failed.

Floor 05

The External Correction

Three resignations. CMS restriction. Congressional investigation. DOJ probe. EU withdrawal. The system corrected from outside because it could not from within.

"Five cracks. One foundation. One direction of failure. The building looked stable until the moment it didn't."

The Structural Rebuild

Intervention A

DMC Governance Lock

Futility recommendations become binding. Sponsor claims of analytical error require mandatory independent biostatistical audit. DMC must reconvene within 30 days. Post-hoc subgroups prohibited as primary evidence.

Intervention B

Interaction Audit Trail

Every substantive sponsor-regulator interaction logged with timestamps and authorship. Advisory committee receives full interaction log 14 days before vote. Concealment becomes structurally impossible.
